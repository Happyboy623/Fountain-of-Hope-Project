Cape Town Fountain of Hope Church

“Pure and undefiled religion before God and the Father is this: to visit orphans and widows in their trouble, and to keep oneself unspotted from the world.”- James 1:27 New King James Version (NKJV)

At Cape Fountain of Hope Church, We believe that church goes beyond the walls of a physical structures on a Sunday morning, but is IT the hands and feet of Jesus Christ that serve the sick, the lost, the poor, the hungry, the orphans, the widows and anyone who is in a helpless situation.

We do meet regularly to worship and fellowship, but most of the work we do for God’s kingdom, happens beyond the walls our sanctuaries.
