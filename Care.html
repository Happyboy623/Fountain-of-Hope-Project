ministry of care

feeding program

Our feeding program/ soup kitchen provide nutritious meals to 200+ children daily. We believe that no child should go to bed hungry, and through this, we strive to make a tangible impact on their lives. Every day, we make it our mission to provide not just sustenance but a hearty meal filled with care and warmth.

home-based care

Compassion and Empathy knows no bounds, and we extend our hands to care for children and adults who are disabled and sick. Through our dedicated volunteers of caregivers, we aim to improve the quality of life for those facing health challenges, ensuring that they feel valued and loved.
