skills development programs

early childhood development

Education is a powerful tool for breaking the cycle of poverty. Our pre-schools caters to children from disadvantaged backgrounds, offering them a safe and nurturing environment to learn and grow. We believe that every child deserves access to quality education, that is why our pre-schools offers a safe and stimulating environment for children, giving them a strong foundation for their education.

after-school learning

At our church, we've created a warm and inviting space for children who may be facing challenges with their schoolwork. We've opened our doors to offer assistance with homework, assignments, and projects, providing a helping hand to those who need it most. Additionally, we're passionate about education, and we've taken the initiative to teach English and Arts to other children, creating a supportive and enriching environment for learning.

youth skills development

Through partnership with CodeTelligence (2019-2021) and VTA, young people are offered basic computer skills as well as advanced tech trainings. The organisations uses our church space to do their trainings for the youth in our communities.
